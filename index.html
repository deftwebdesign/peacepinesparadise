Find the Retreat

Located in the pines of Parks, Arizona — an easy drive from Flagstaff, Sedona, and the Grand Canyon.

Parks, Arizona

The retreat sits on Fallow Deer Road in the small mountain community of Parks — surrounded by Kaibab National Forest at an elevation of ~7,000 ft. Cool summers, snowy winters, and unbeatable stargazing year-round.
